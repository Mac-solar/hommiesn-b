THERMOMETRY

Twemperature is the property of a body which decides which way heat will flow when the two bodies are in thermal contact, with each other.
Hence temperature is the measure of how hot or cold a body is.
Temperature is the measure of the average kinetic energy of an object. Two objects at the same temperature do not necessarily have thesame energy because the internal energy of an object depends on its mass as well as temperature
